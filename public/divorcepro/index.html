Project Overview

DivorcePro - Online Divorce Platform

DivorcePro is an online platform that simplifies the divorce process

The problem it solves is the bureaucracy and high cost of traditional divorce proceedings. Instead of dealing with expensive lawyers and confusing paperwork, clients have a clear, automated flow supported by legal assistants.

The timing is relevant because there's growing demand for digital legal services, with document recognition, electronic filing, and remote assistance expanding across the US.

The primary target audience is married adults in Florida who want an uncontested, fast, and affordable divorce.

Insights & Opportunities

Analysis of the online divorce market and strategic positioning

1. Market Patterns

Guided questionnaire + form generation

Almost all offer guided questionnaire + form generation and filing instructions.

Few assume direct e-filing

Few assume direct e-filing and e-notarization as part of the flow.

Fast Florida Divorce stands out

Highlights RON + e-filing in the state as a competitive differentiator.

2. Experience Gaps

Visual status tracking absent

"Status tracking" visual of the case and progress panel are not evident in most competitors.

Friction reported in reviews

Friction is reported in reviews (unclear guidelines, extra charges, etc.).

Dashboard with steps 1-5

Strengthen the dashboard with steps 1-5, deadlines, and alerts, as your PRD proposes.

3. Actionable Differentiations for FL

RON + E-filing Integrated

Integrate RON (NotaryCam/Proof) and e-filing (via USLegalPro) in the same flow, reducing time and anxiety as a competitive advantage.

County Checklist

Highlight counties without a hearing (like Lee), offering clarity on the specific process for each region.

Cost Transparency

Clear messaging on costs (court fee ~US$400) from the start, avoiding surprises reported by competitors.
